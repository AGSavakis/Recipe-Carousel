  1. Place large cookie sheet in oven and heat oven to 475 degrees F.
  2. In a very large bowl toss together spices, black pepper, garlic, chicken and oil.
  3. Add peppers (cut into 1/4 - 1/2" strips) and greens to spice mixture and toss until peppers and greens are coated.
  4. Dump ingredients onto the preheated cookie sheet. Turn occasionally so ingredients don't stick or burn. Bake about 20-30 minutes or until chicken and peppers are cooked.

Serves four.
